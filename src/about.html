About Us

Capturing Stories, Creating Memories

At BehindTheLens, we capture moments, tell stories, and bring your vision to life through photography and videography with creativity and precision.

Our Vision

To be a force in South African creative media by producing world-class photography, videography, and design, while breaking barriers for women in media and empowering the next generation of creatives.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative and impactful visual content while creating opportunities for creatives in underrepresented areas, inspiring and empowering the next generation to thrive in photography, videography, and design.

Always Prepared to Frame Your Story

Meet the Founder

Aphiwokuhle Moyo

Aphiwokuhle Moyo is the founder of BehindTheLens Pty Ltd, a dynamic female-led media company established in 2018. With a diploma in Photography and years of experience in visual storytelling, she has built a brand that thrives on creativity, professionalism, and empowerment. Her work has been featured in national publications and even premiered on MTV Base, inspiring the next generation of creatives while breaking barriers for women in media.
