Meet The Team

Founded in 2018, Behindthelens Pty Ltd is a female-led media company based in Port Shepstone, specialising in photography, videography, graphic design, and premium prints. From small-town roots, we’ve grown to serve clients across South Africa, delivering creative and professional content for both personal and corporate projects. Recognised in leading publications and nominated for the Sebenza Women Awards, our work has even premiered on MTV Base. With collaborations spanning universities, beauty brands, and event companies, Behindthelens continues to blend creativity and professionalism to capture moments that inspire and last a lifetime.
