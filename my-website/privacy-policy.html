Privacy Policy

At BehindTheLens, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our photography services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you contact us, book a session, or subscribe to our newsletter. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Information about your photography preferences
  • Any files or images you share with us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and booking requests
  • Provide our photography services
  • Send you updates, promotions, or newsletters (if you opted in)

3. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except when required by law or to fulfill our services (e.g., sharing your photos with contracted editors).

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To do so, please contact us via info@behindthelenssa.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
