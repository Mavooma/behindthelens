Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how BehindTheLens uses cookies and similar technologies on our website to enhance your browsing experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, login information, and user activity.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • To improve website performance and functionality.
  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns.
  • To personalize your experience on our website.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the website.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and track effectiveness.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies, but this may affect the functionality of some parts of the website.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services, such as analytics providers, to place cookies on our website. These cookies are governed by the respective third-party privacy policies.

6. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with the date of revision.
